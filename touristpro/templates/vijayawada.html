Kanakadurga Ammavaru

Kanaka Durga Temple, also known as Sri Kanaka Durgamma Devasthanam is a Hindu temple dedicated to Kanaka Durga. The deity in this temple is also popularly referred as Kanaka Durga. The temple is located in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India on the Indrakeeladri Hills on the banks of Krishna River Kanaka Durga Temple is synonymous with Vijayawada. It is mentioned in the sacred texts. It is mentioned in the Hindu scriptures that the deity in the Sri Kanaka Durgamma Devasthanam is regarded as Swayambhu or self-manifested, hence considered very powerful
